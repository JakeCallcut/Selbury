# Cookie Policy

*Last updated: September 21, 2024*

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and allow us to understand how you use our site.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:
- Security and authentication
- Session management
- Load balancing
- Access to secure areas

### Analytics Cookies
We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our services. We use:
- **Plausible Analytics**: A privacy-focused analytics service that doesn't use cookies for tracking
- First-party analytics data to understand user behaviour

### Functional Cookies
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation:
- Language preferences
- User interface preferences
- Remember login status
- Form data persistence

### Marketing Cookies
With your consent, we may use marketing cookies to:
- Show relevant advertisements
- Measure advertising effectiveness
- Retarget website visitors
- Personalise marketing content

## Cookie Categories

### Strictly Necessary
These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off.

**Examples:**
- Authentication tokens

- Security cookies
- Load balancer cookies

### Performance and Analytics
These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website.

**Examples:**
- Page view tracking
- User journey analysis
- Performance monitoring
- Error reporting

### Functional
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation.

**Examples:**
- Language selection
- Region selection
- User preferences
- Accessibility settings

### Targeting and Advertising
These cookies may be set through our site by advertising partners.

**Examples:**
- Ad personalisation
- Cross-site tracking
- Conversion tracking

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

### Our Cookie Consent Manager
When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customise your preferences
- Learn more about each cookie category

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

## Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different retention periods:
- **Session cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies**: Remain for a set period or until manually deleted
- **Analytics cookies**: Typically retained for 24 months
- **Marketing cookies**: Typically retained for 12 months

## Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:
- Be informed about our use of cookies
- Consent to non-essential cookies
- Withdraw your consent at any time
- Access information about cookies we've set
- Delete cookies from your device
